January 15, 2024

LumaWave secures Tulane's PPOC fund to build proof-of-concept prototype point-of-care photoacoustic system

LumaWave's team was awarded the Tulane PPOC fund to develop a proof of concept prototype of a low-cost, point-of-care photoacoustic imaging prototype for assessment of tissue ischemia. The award marks a key milestone in translating fundamental research from the Bayer Lab into a viable clinical solution.
