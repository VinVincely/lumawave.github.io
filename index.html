Making point-of-care assessment of tissue vitality a reality

THE CHALLENGE

Healthcare providers need a rapid, non-invasive, point-of-care method to assess deep tissue hypoxia.

Currently, there are no portable methods that can spot assess tissue hypoxia. Clinical systems are either expensive, inacessible or impresise in measurements of tissue oxygenation.

THE SOLUTION

PA Probe

Utilizes photoacoustic technology to assess functional information using point-of-care ultrasound hardware, enabling physicians with rapid, real-time measurements of tissue oxygenation.


PA probe is currently in the research and development phase and not available for sale in any country.

Our Benefits

Physicians

The PA probe allows physicians an instant spot-assessment of deep tissue oxygenation, allowing sensitive real-time measurement of hypoxia or early stage ischemia.

Payers

With competitive pricing to other point-of-care ultrasound systems, the PA probe allows easy assessment of hypoxia reduces healthcare costs, demonstrate effectiveness of therapies and reduce complications.

Patients

The PA probe is a solution that provides patients a peace of mind as their physicians are empowered with critical clinical indicators.
