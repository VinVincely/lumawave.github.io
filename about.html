Meet the passionate team behind LumaWave, combining innovation, engineering expertise, and a shared mission to transform diagnostic imaging and advance accessible healthcare for all.

Carolyn L. Bayer

Chief Technology Officer & Co-Founder

Dr. Carolyn Bayer, an Associate Professor of Biomedical Engineering at Tulane University, develops advanced imaging technologies to improve women’s reproductive health. Her work combines bioinstrumentation, optical imaging, and physiological modeling to address conditions such as preeclampsia and uterine dysfunction, driving interdisciplinary research toward clinically impactful solutions.
